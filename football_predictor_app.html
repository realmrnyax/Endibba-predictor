Auto-Fetch (Major Leagues Only)

Free API covers 12 major leagues/cups (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, Serie A, Eredivisie, Primeira Liga, Championship, Brazil Serie A, Champions League, World Cup, Euros). Fills scoring form, half/full-time splits, recent points. Corners, shots, possession, referee, set pieces, and squad/context stay manual — not available on the free tier, even for these leagues. Get a free key at football-data.org/client/register.
